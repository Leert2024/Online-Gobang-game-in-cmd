在cmd窗口运行的 局域网内联机的 五子棋
intra-LAN Gobang game in cmd

一款用Python编写的简单的五子棋，可在局域网内联机进行游戏。
This is a simple Gobang game programmed using Python. Playing inside LAN is possible.

先手或后手随机决定。
Whether you are offensive or defensive is randomly decided.

按↑↓←→键以决定落子位置，按s以落子，按o退出游戏。
Press ↑↓←→ to decide where to place a chess. Press s to settle the chess. Press o to exit the game.

当5枚相同棋子连成一行，游戏结束，该方胜利。
The game ends and you win when 5 chess of your colour form a line.
